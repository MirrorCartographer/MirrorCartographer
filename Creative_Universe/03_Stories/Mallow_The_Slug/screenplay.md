# Screenplay: Mallow - Three-Minute Animatic

00:00 Title. The moon hangs over a wet forest floor. Mallow glows faintly.
00:10 Ordinary world. Mallow lives slowly. Fast creatures rush past.
00:28 Inciting incident. The moon separates into glowing fragments in the pond.
00:45 Call. A firefly asks for help. Fast wings failed.
01:00 Fear. The journey is impossibly long.
01:15 Discovery. Mallow's trail catches moonlight.
01:32 Crossing. A leaf becomes a boat. Rain begins. A heron shadow hunts brightness.
01:52 Crisis. The trail breaks, then every droplet glows.
02:10 Climb. Mallow climbs the mushroom mountain.
02:35 Return. Moonlight follows the trail upward.
02:52 Resolution. Dawn arrives. The shining path remains.
03:00 End. Slow is still a way through.
