## Purpose

Use this when AI, automation, or an AI-assisted human changes a work product, care plan, record, decision path, communication, code path, eligibility path, or other artifact that may affect money, care, access, safety, trust, or institutional responsibility.

This template does not diagnose, give legal advice, or replace professional review. It creates a structured record of transformation, evidence, missingness, and consequence.
