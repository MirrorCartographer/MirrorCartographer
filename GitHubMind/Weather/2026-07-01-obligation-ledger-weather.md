# Weather — Obligation Ledger

**Date:** 2026-07-01  
**Attractor:** Continuity  
**Status:** Public-safe weather artifact  
**Revision:** v1.0

## External weather

The current governance weather is moving from static documentation toward runtime evidence, traceability, and lifecycle accountability.

Three signals matter here:

1. **Healthcare agent governance is becoming lifecycle governance.** Recent healthcare-agent work describes identity registries, runtime policy enforcement, kill-switch triggers, decommissioning, credential revocation, and audit logging as necessary control-plane layers.
2. **Runtime compliance is becoming trace-based.** Recent compliance-verification work proposes monitors that intercept tool invocations and model outputs, representing consent, purpose limitation, minimization, and erasure as predicates over execution traces.
3. **Responsibility requires provenance and attribution.** Recent agentic AI provenance work argues that responsibility must be computable, traceable, and interventionable across the full agentic lifecycle.

## Compression

**Decision Record + Action Certificate + Missingness = Obligation Ledger pressure.**

Any action taken under incomplete evidence, delegated authority, consent boundary, or external effect creates a residue. That residue must either be closed, transferred, retired, or carried forward.

## Income lane weather

Organizations adopting agentic systems will need more than model evaluations. They will need evidence that obligations survive automation:

- follow-up duties;
- unresolved claims;
- escalation duties;
- deletion or correction duties;
- review duties;
- rollback and recovery duties.

This creates a plausible service lane: **AI Obligation Trace Review**.

## Medical / social-care weather

Care and social-support systems often fail at handoff, follow-up, documentation correction, and unresolved responsibility. AI may amplify this if generated summaries appear complete while hiding open obligations.

A **Care Obligation Ledger** could help by making unfinished obligations visible while preserving clinician or case-worker authority.
