# Weather — Provenance Carry-Forward

## Attractor

Continuity.

## External weather

The external environment is moving toward operational AI governance, not static trust statements. Recent work on production-agent governance emphasizes runtime control planes, path-dependent policy evaluation, composite authority, interruption, and audit evidence. Healthcare-agent governance work similarly emphasizes identity registries, PHI-bounded context and memory, runtime policy enforcement, kill-switch triggers, lifecycle management, credential revocation, and audit logging.

The market and regulatory signal is that agentic systems need evidence that follows the action path. A policy that exists outside the path is not enough.

## Care and social-care weather

Healthcare and social-care AI tools are increasingly being used for documentation, summarization, scheduling, coding, and administrative support. This creates real value through reduced documentation load and improved coordination, but it also creates specific risk when summaries transform observations into stronger claims, omit uncertainty, or introduce fabricated content.

The relevant care-support opportunity is not autonomous diagnosis. It is lineage-preserving documentation support: keeping observations, uncertainty, review status, correction history, and authority boundaries visible as records move between patient, caregiver, clinician, administrative, and social-care views.
