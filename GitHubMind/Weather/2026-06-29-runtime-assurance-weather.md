## Current weather

The public signal continues moving toward runtime assurance.

Common elements:

- ongoing risk assessment
- independent validation
- human oversight for automated decisions
- runtime policy enforcement
- audit logging
- lifecycle management
- decommissioning or limiting high-risk systems
- source fidelity in documentation
- escalation and human intervention controls

## Relevant public signals

- Financial AI governance: RBI draft guidance reported by Reuters on 2026-06-24 emphasizes ongoing model risk assessment, independent validation, enhanced controls, limits on use, possible decommissioning, and human oversight for automated decision-making.
- Healthcare agent governance: 2026 healthcare agent lifecycle work emphasizes identity/persona registries, PHI-bounded memory, runtime policy enforcement, kill switches, credential revocation, audit logging, and lifecycle management.
- Social care documentation: 2026 reporting on Ada Lovelace Institute findings shows that AI transcription/summarization can save time but can also introduce harmful distortions into official care records.
- Interface oversight: 2026 human-oversight-by-design work emphasizes escalation thresholds, risk signalling, accessibility, semantic fidelity, and traceable audit logs for high-stakes generated interfaces.
- Medical AI agents: 2026 ethics-by-design proposals emphasize auditable reasoning, explicit human override conditions, patient preference profiles, oversight tools, benchmarking, and regulatory sandboxes.
