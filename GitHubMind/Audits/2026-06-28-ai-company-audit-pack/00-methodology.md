# 00 — Methodology

Date: 2026-06-28
Privacy: PUBLIC-SAFE
Revision: v0.1
Claim boundary: Public-source audit simulation. No private logs, employee claims, confidential financials, security testing, or internal system access.

## Audit frame

This audit treats each company as a public signal system. The object of analysis is not only what the company says, but the gap between:

1. product claims,
2. safety/governance claims,
3. developer experience,
4. economic model,
5. public incidents/criticisms,
6. missing evidence,
7. opportunities for Mirror Cartographer-style structured transformation work.

## Evidence labels

SOURCE: public source directly supports the statement.

INFERENCE: public evidence supports a reasonable conclusion, but the company has not stated it exactly.

HYPOTHESIS: possible opportunity or risk requiring testing.

UNKNOWN: information not available from public evidence.

## Confidence labels

HIGH: multiple reliable public sources or official source.

MEDIUM: one reliable source, or multiple partial sources.

LOW: weak public evidence, early signal, or uncertain interpretation.

## Audit dimensions

- Product surface: what the user experiences.
- Architecture surface: what the public docs imply about systems, tools, memory, agents, APIs, and evaluation.
- Governance surface: safety frameworks, policy documents, public commitments, public criticisms.
- Economic surface: pricing, enterprise wedge, usage economics, cost pressure, buyer profile.
- Failure surface: hallucination, autonomy, misuse, privacy, legal exposure, overclaiming, brittle evaluation.
- Opportunity surface: where a third-party structured-audit artifact could create value.

## Mirror Cartographer contribution

Mirror Cartographer’s differentiator is not a larger model. It is a transformation layer:

- source-to-claim mapping,
- uncertainty tracking,
- contradiction detection,
- revision memory,
- symbolic/semantic compression,
- public-safe artifact generation,
- human-facing orientation rather than only model-facing optimization.

## Non-claims

This pack does not claim:

- that any company hired Mirror Cartographer,
- that private systems were inspected,
- that vulnerabilities were tested,
- that financials are complete,
- that medical advice is being given,
- that social-care interventions are validated as products.
