## Practical lane 2: medical or social-care support

Candidate care-support artifact:

Care Review Trigger Card.

Short description:

A public-safe card that flags when a medical, veterinary, or social-care summary needs professional review before reliance.

Boundary:

- observation support only;
- no diagnosis;
- no treatment instruction;
- no emergency triage replacement;
- clinician, veterinarian, or social worker remains authoritative.

Evidence direction:

CARE shows that calibrated omission and hallucination flags for medical summaries are technically plausible. Social-care reporting shows that AI summaries can produce harmful errors when insufficiently reviewed.
