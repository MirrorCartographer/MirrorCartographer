## Why this attractor

The repository has accumulated many governance primitives: authority, delegation, assurance, interruption, recovery, reversibility, custody, governance state, and runtime control.

The strongest need is compression into a usable boundary that can decide whether a workflow is ready for bounded reliance.

## External research signal

Fresh public signals reviewed on 2026-06-30 point toward operational proof:

- Healthcare agent governance research describes lifecycle management, identity registries, PHI-bounded context, runtime policy enforcement, kill-switch triggers, decommissioning, credential revocation, and audit logging.
- Trace-based assurance research frames agentic failures as execution-level problems requiring message-action traces, contracts, replay, stress testing, fault injection, containment metrics, and runtime governance.
- AGENTSAFE emphasizes design, runtime, and audit controls, interruptibility, human escalation, semantic telemetry, dynamic authorization, anomaly detection, and accountable assurance.
- Runtime-guardrail research argues that governance standards must be translated into observable, determinate, time-sensitive controls at execution time.
- 2026 enterprise coverage reports warning that weak autonomous-agent governance may force significant rollback by 2027.
