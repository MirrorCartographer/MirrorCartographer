## Why this attractor

The repository has been building increasingly strong governance structure: assurance, reversibility, interruption, recovery, custody, readiness, deployment proof, and explanation layers.

The contradiction now is that explanation increases accessibility while also increasing the chance of semantic drift.

A plain-language layer can help a person understand. It can also erase uncertainty.

A beautiful layer can make a system legible. It can also make weak evidence feel stronger than it is.
