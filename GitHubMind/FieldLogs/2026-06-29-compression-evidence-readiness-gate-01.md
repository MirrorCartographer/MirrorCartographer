## Compression found

Many current governance signals compress into one practical question:

Is this claim ready to move, or should it be parked, revised, used only as draft, made audit-ready, or nominated as a Museum candidate?

## Comparison against existing GitHub Mind

### Existing layer: Evidence Chain

Evidence Chain records how claims, transformations, consequences, revisions, and later validations connect across time.

Gap found:
A chain can preserve continuity without deciding what any link is allowed to do next.

### Existing layer: Fluent Ambiguity Scorecard

The scorecard detects when AI output sounds useful while hiding weak sourcing, missing uncertainty, privacy exposure, or consequence-bearing inference.

Gap found:
A scorecard can identify weakness without assigning a next action.

### New layer: Evidence Readiness Gate

The gate gives every reviewed object a status:

1. Park
2. Revise
3. Use as draft
4. Audit-ready
5. Museum candidate

## Forces

Dominant force:

Evidence must increasingly become actionable without becoming reckless.

The gate is a restraint mechanism. It slows down premature action while preserving useful movement.

## Practical lane 1: income

The strongest concrete path is still a narrow service rather than a broad platform:

AI Evidence Readiness Review

Deliverables:
- readiness status for each major claim
- Evidence Chain summary
- Fluent Ambiguity score
- missingness report
- revision recommendations
- executive-ready risk note

Potential buyer pain:
Organizations are adopting AI faster than they can explain, audit, or defend the transformations those systems make.

## Practical lane 2: medical or social care

The strongest care-adjacent direction remains documentation quality and continuity, not diagnosis.

Useful support surface:
- appointment preparation
- separating observation from interpretation
- documenting changes over time
- preserving uncertainty
- identifying what must be reviewed by a clinician

Boundary:
This is not diagnosis, treatment, triage, or clinical decision automation.
