The strongest care-adjacent use is not diagnosis. It is documentation safety.

- direct observation,
- user-reported history,
- AI summary,
- clinician-confirmed finding,
- unverified inference,
- action recommendation,
- expired or superseded context.

This supports continuity and review without claiming medical authority.
