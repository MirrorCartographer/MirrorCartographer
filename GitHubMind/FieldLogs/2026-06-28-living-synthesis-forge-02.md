## Transformation Record v0.1

A Transformation Record captures one AI-mediated change.

Required fields:

- artifact_id
- original_intent
- input_context
- AI_action
- human_action
- changed_claims
- changed_decisions
- changed_risks
- evidence_used
- evidence_missing
- source_boundary
- privacy_boundary
- uncertainty_boundary
- review_required
- revision_path
- final_status

## New metric set

### 1. Transformation Density

Definition: number of meaningful changes per artifact divided by artifact size or review time.
Use: identifies high-change outputs that deserve review even when they read smoothly.

### 2. Claim Drift

Definition: distance between final statement and available evidence.
Use: detects polished overreach.

### 3. Evidence Entropy

Definition: scatter and weakness of the evidence base supporting a claim set.
Use: shows when confidence is being carried by fluency instead of support.

### 4. Action Risk Delta

Definition: how much safer or riskier the next real-world action becomes after AI involvement.
Use: separates harmless writing from operational impact.

### 5. Orientation Gain

Definition: measurable increase in a human's ability to explain the situation, choose the next action, and name uncertainty.
Use: makes human understanding part of the output, not an afterthought.

## Income lane

Offer name: AI Transformation Audit

Buyer pain:
- AI outputs are spreading faster than review capacity.
- Teams do not know what changed.
- Governance documents are often detached from actual workflows.
- Legal, medical, financial, code, and operational outputs all need clearer review trails.

Small sellable service:
- Audit 10 AI-generated artifacts.
- Return Transformation Records.
- Score unsupported claims, missing evidence, decision impact, and revision priority.
- Deliver an executive summary plus revised artifacts.

Potential entry buyers:
- small law firms using AI drafting,
- clinics or care coordinators using AI notes,
- consultants using AI deliverables,
- software teams using AI code generation,
- nonprofits using AI grants/reports,
- founders using AI for strategy documents.

## Medical and social-care lane

Current practical translation: Care Communication Audit.

Purpose:
- turn scattered symptoms, appointments, messages, observations, and care questions into a structured communication packet;
- preserve uncertainty;
- prevent AI from pretending to diagnose;
- improve the next professional interaction.

Public-safe product form:
- timeline,
- symptom map,
- medication and intervention log,
- red-flag checklist sourced from reputable medical guidance,
- question list for clinician or veterinarian,
- evidence/missingness labels.

Boundary:
This is support for communication and organization, not diagnosis or treatment.
