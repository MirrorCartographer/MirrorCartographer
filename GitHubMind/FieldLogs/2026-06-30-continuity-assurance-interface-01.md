## Core upgrade

**Assurance must be inspectable at the point of reliance.**

A label hidden inside documentation does not protect a user, reviewer, clinician, manager, or institution at the moment they act. The claim, source, authority, missingness, privacy boundary, action ceiling, revision trigger, and exit path must appear at the decision surface.

**AI Assurance Interface Review**

Why it is realistic:

## Practical lane 2: medical/social-care

Best evidence-aligned support lane:

**Care Continuity Interface Contract**

Purpose:

Support observation continuity and clinician-facing communication without claiming diagnosis or treatment authority.

Allowed:

- organize observations;
- separate source from interpretation;
- preserve uncertainty;
- identify missing information;
- prepare questions for clinicians;
- track revision history.

Not allowed:

- diagnosis;
- prescription;
- emergency triage;
- replacing medical, veterinary, legal, or social-care professionals;
- silently converting AI drafts into official records.
