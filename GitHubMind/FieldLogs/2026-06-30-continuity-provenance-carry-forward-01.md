## Core upgrade

**A claim that survives reuse must carry its provenance with it.**

If source, claim, privacy, missingness, revision, confidence, and authority labels are removed during reuse, the derivative artifact must be marked as unverified derivative material.

## New primitive

**Provenance Carry-Forward Ledger**

Purpose:

Track whether source, claim, privacy, missingness, revision, and authority labels survive transformation from one artifact into another.
