Maintain GitHub Mind as the living workspace by adding one public-safe evolution across Genesis, Weather, Forces, Museum, and Field Logs.

## Attractor chosen

Contradiction.

## Why this attractor

The repository has recently strengthened provenance, verification, claim portability, layer consistency, recovery, and deployment proof. The strongest current contradiction is that a verified artifact can still act without permission.

This matters because the outside field is moving toward AI systems that do more than answer: they summarize care conversations, write drafts, coordinate workflows, transact, call tools, send messages, and affect records.

## Repository comparison

Existing primitives already cover:

- claim boundaries;
- evidence boundaries;

- verification surfaces;
- interruption;
- recovery;
- governance states;
- deployment proof;
- layer drift;
- claim cards.

New gap identified:

**Authorization must remain visible after verification.**

A claim card says what a claim is. A verification surface lets the next recipient inspect it. A consent authority envelope says what the claim or artifact is allowed to do.

## Core upgrade

**Verification is not authorization.**

A verified claim may still be unauthorized to act. A sourced summary may still be unauthorized to enter a record. A provenance-rich artifact may still be unauthorized to travel into a new context.
