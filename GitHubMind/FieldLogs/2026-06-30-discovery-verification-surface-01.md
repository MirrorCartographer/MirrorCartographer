# Field Log — Discovery / Verification Surface Protocol

## Run focus

Maintain the GitHub Mind as the living workspace by adding one public-safe evolution across Genesis, Weather, Forces, Museum, and Field Logs.

## Attractor chosen

Discovery.

## Why this attractor

The previous verified direction focused on continuity through the Provenance Carry-Forward Ledger: claims that survive reuse must carry their provenance with them.

The strongest next discovery is that provenance must not merely be carried internally. It must become inspectable by the next recipient at the exact place where the artifact is trusted, copied, summarized, exported, presented, or used.

## Repository comparison

Existing direction:

- claim boundaries are explicit;
- assurance should appear at the point of reliance;
- layer drift should be detected;
- provenance should survive movement;
- deployment readiness should require proof;
- interruption, recovery, rollback, custody, and governance states have been modeled.

Gap found:

- provenance can survive inside a ledger while remaining invisible to the next recipient;
- metadata can be stripped;
- summaries can detach from their source and uncertainty;
- care-adjacent records can become influential without visible review state;
- polished outputs can hide whether they are original, derivative, superseded, or unverified.

## Core upgrade

**Provenance that cannot be inspected by the next recipient is not yet operational provenance.**

## New primitive

**Verification Surface Protocol**

Purpose:

Expose source, claim, privacy, missingness, revision, authority, derivative status, supersession state, and correction path at the surface where reliance occurs.

## Practical lane 1: income

Best current commercialization path:

**AI Verification Surface Review**

Use case:

Organizations using AI to produce reports, summaries, internal policies, external claims, client deliverables, product pages, knowledge-base entries, governance documents, or care-adjacent notes need proof that their outputs expose enough provenance for safe review and correction.

Deliverables:

- artifact surface inventory;
- provenance visibility audit;
- derivative-risk map;
- missing-verification register;
- claim card template;
- correction and supersession workflow;
- implementation roadmap.

## Practical lane 2: medical and social-care

Best evidence-aligned support lane:

**Care Summary Verification Card**

Purpose:

Preserve observation source, review state, uncertainty, authority boundary, correction history, and supersession state when care-related information moves between patient, caregiver, clinician, administrative, referral, and social-care contexts.

Boundary:

This supports communication, continuity, review, and correction. It does not diagnose, treat, triage, or replace clinician or qualified care-professional judgment.

## Missingness label

This pass did not implement cryptographic signatures, C2PA metadata, watermarking, checksum manifests, or automated repository-wide verification scans. The primitive is conceptual and manual until claim IDs, export metadata, and verification cards are implemented.

## Revision label

Revise if:

- artifact exports add embedded metadata;
- C2PA, watermarking, or checksum tooling is implemented;
