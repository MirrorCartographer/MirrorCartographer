## Weather label

The public weather is moving toward trace-first governance:

- agent runs represented as action traces
- audit trails as lifecycle ledgers
- observability as a compliance substrate
- healthcare agent fleets requiring identity, PHI-bounded memory, runtime controls, and decommissioning
- professional services firms expanding AI assurance capacity

## Forces label

Dominant force:

> Evidence produced during operation is becoming more valuable than policy written before operation.

Policies still matter, but they increasingly need traces, logs, attestations, and review pathways to prove they were followed.
