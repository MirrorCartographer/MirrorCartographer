## Museum purpose

This museum entry freezes the **Layer Drift Sentinel** as an idea worth preserving even before implementation.

It should not be promoted as:

- a completed safety system;
- a medical tool;
- a legal compliance guarantee;
- an enterprise certification;
- a diagnostic or treatment workflow;
- evidence that Mirror Cartographer has solved AI governance.

## Why preserve it

The repository has been building stronger governance machinery. The current risk is that the machinery becomes unreadable or gets translated into simpler layers that accidentally change meaning.

The Layer Drift Sentinel preserves a clean rule:

**Different audience, same claim boundary.**

## Artifact value

This primitive is valuable because it names a subtle failure mode:

A system can be technically careful and publicly careless at the same time.

A care-support summary can be emotionally helpful and clinically overreaching at the same time.

A sales description can be accurate in spirit and false in operational consequence at the same time.

## Kept out of scope

- unsupported performance promises.
