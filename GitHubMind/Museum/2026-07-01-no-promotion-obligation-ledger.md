## Artifact

**Obligation Ledger Protocol**

A primitive for tracking duties that remain after claims, decisions, consent boundaries, actions, incidents, handoffs, and unresolved missingness.

## Why this belongs in the Museum

This artifact captures a structural insight before turning it into a public claim of capability.

It is not yet:

- software;
- a compliance product;
- a medical device;
- a legal record system;
- a replacement for clinician, case-worker, auditor, or governance authority.

It is a design primitive.

## Preserved insight

**Completion is not the same thing as closure.**

A workflow may complete while obligations remain active. The repository now has a way to name that residue.
