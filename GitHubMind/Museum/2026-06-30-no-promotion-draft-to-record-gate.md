# Museum Decision — No Promotion: Draft-to-Record Gate

## Decision

Do not promote **Draft-to-Record Gate** to Museum yet.

## Reason

The primitive is strongly aligned with current evidence and the existing GitHub Mind architecture, but it has not yet survived repeated application across different workflows.

## Promotion criteria

Promote only if the gate proves useful across at least three distinct public-safe documentation contexts, such as:

1. AI meeting summaries.
2. Healthcare or veterinary continuity notes.
3. Social-care or support-service records.
4. Governance/audit reports.
5. Mirror Cartographer reflective session artifacts.

## Required evidence before promotion

- The gate prevents at least one category of source distortion.
- Human reviewers can understand and apply the labels quickly.
- It reduces ambiguity between draft, interpretation, and official record.
- It does not create excessive administrative burden.
- It preserves privacy boundaries.
