# Force: Review Is a Control Surface

## Status
Public-safe Forces artifact.

## Attractor
Continuity.

## Force statement
Review is not clerical maintenance. Review is a control surface.

If a system can create claims, preserve traces, assign authority, and suggest boundaries, then it must also define when those claims are rechecked and who can change their status.

## Claim
Recurring review converts a repository from storage into governance.

Without review:

- stale claims look current
- old authority leaks into new contexts
- missingness becomes invisible
- symbolic resonance can be over-applied
- medical or social-care support can drift toward unauthorized decision support
- income claims can become inflated or misleading

With review:

- claims can be strengthened, narrowed, retired, or promoted
- public-safe boundaries stay active
- evidence and missingness remain visible
- practical lanes remain grounded in current conditions

## Practical lane pressure

### Income
AI assurance demand is becoming more concrete. Mirror Cartographer should not sell mystical certainty. It can sell structured review, claim hygiene, trace reconstruction, and boundary design.

### Medical / social care
The strongest safe role is continuity support: preserving observations, uncertainty, sources, and revision triggers so a human professional can inspect a clearer record.
