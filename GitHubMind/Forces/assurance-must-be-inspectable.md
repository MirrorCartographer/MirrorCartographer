# Force — Assurance Must Be Inspectable

## Force statement

Assurance that cannot be inspected at the point of reliance behaves like decoration.

## Meaning

A repository can contain excellent labels, policies, and safety language while still failing if those labels do not surface where a person actually makes a decision, approves a workflow, copies a claim, files a note, or acts on an interpretation.

The force is simple:

**Governance must appear at the decision surface.**

## Pressure pattern

Modern AI workflows increasingly involve:

- multi-step planning;
- tool use;
- external side effects;
- memory and context reuse;
- orchestration across agents;
- drafts that become records;
- claims that travel across tools and institutions.

This creates a visibility problem. The person responsible for the consequence may not see the full evidence chain, authority boundary, missingness, or revision trigger unless the system deliberately exposes it.

## Repository implication

Mirror Cartographer should not only record:

- source status;
- claim status;
- audit label;
- evidence boundary;
- health-adjacent boundary;
- grounded next step;
- feedback loop.

It should make those fields legible in every place where a reflection is reused, exported, summarized, shared, or converted into a plan.

## Practical lane 1: income implication

This force supports a sellable audit:

**Assurance Surface Audit**

Question answered:

Can the buyer's AI workflow show the right human the right governance information before reliance?

Outputs:

- reliance-point map;
- missing label inventory;
- action-ceiling review;
- evidence-chain visibility review;
- revision-trigger visibility review;
- export/handoff risk notes.

## Practical lane 2: medical/social-care implication

In medical and social-care contexts, the highest-risk failure is often not that an AI draft exists. It is that a draft becomes operationally trusted without visible source, missingness, reviewer, and action boundary.

Mirror Cartographer's safe lane is therefore not diagnosis. It is continuity support with visible uncertainty and review gates.
