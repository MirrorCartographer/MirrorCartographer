# Force: Continuation Must Be Earned

## Force statement

In AI-assisted systems, permission to begin is weaker than permission to continue.

A system, claim, workflow, or artifact should continue only while its evidence, context, oversight, and risk posture remain sufficient for the action being supported.

## Pressure created by this force

This force pushes the GitHub Mind away from static artifacts and toward living governance objects.

It pressures every claim to carry:

- a review condition,
- a stop condition,
- a reversal condition,
- an escalation owner,
- and a decommission path.

## Why it matters for Mirror Cartographer

Mirror Cartographer works near meaning, emotion, memory, interpretation, care navigation, and AI-mediated reflection. These domains are vulnerable to overreach. A system may start as documentation support and drift into decision support or unauthorized intervention unless continuation is governed explicitly.

## Practical consequence

Add an Exit Authority section to consequential artifacts before promotion to Museum.

Minimum fields:

1. Stop condition
2. Human override path
3. Escalation owner
4. Reversal condition
5. Decommission condition
6. Audit record requirement
7. Revision trigger
