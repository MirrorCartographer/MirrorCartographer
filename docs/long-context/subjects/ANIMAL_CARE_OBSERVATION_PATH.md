## Why this belongs in Mirror Cartographer

Animal care became one of the strongest real-world test cases for the system.

It requires:

- subtle observation
- pattern tracking
- behavior interpretation
- symptom timing
- uncertainty management
- vet communication
- emotional intensity without false certainty
- practical next steps

## Signal categories

### Body signal

Examples:

- eye swelling
- appetite change
- vomiting
- breathing changes
- lymph node enlargement
- skin/scab changes
- energy level
- gait or circling behavior

### Behavior signal

Examples:

- hiding
- pacing
- spinning
- resting more
- seeking contact
- reduced interest in food
- new-place stress

### Environment signal

Examples:

- travel
- flight
- hotel or new location
- weather / heat
- routine disruption
- other animals in household

### Care signal

Examples:

- medication timing
- side effects
- vet visit notes
- specialist recommendations
- follow-up questions

## Boundary statement

Mirror Cartographer can help organize what happened.

It cannot tell whether an animal has a disease, whether a medication caused an event, or what treatment is correct.

It can produce:

- a vet-ready summary
- a timeline
- a question list
- a symptom log
- an uncertainty map
