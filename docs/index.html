Foundation Intelligence Architecture

A continuity-preserving intelligence system designed to survive changes in models, interfaces, databases, tools, and applications.

The more the system changes internally and externally, the more important it becomes to preserve the mapping between what it is, what it remembers, and how it appears.
Reader / PerceptionReads final language, drafts, differences, slang, typos, code, locale, country context, foreground, background, silence, and compression.
AttentionSelects the foreground and the smallest relevant background without flattening the field.
Intelligence KernelPerceive → Attend → Interpret → Reason → Plan → Execute → Reflect → Learn.
Knowledge, Memory, and EvidenceStores relationships, provenance, decisions, symbols, project state, and explicit claim status.
Translation BoundaryMaintains fidelity between internal state and external expression.
Model RouterUses hosted, open-weight, local, specialist, deterministic, and eventually owned models as replaceable engines.
ApplicationsMirror Cartographer, the Reader, research, creative systems, discovery tools, and future products.
