## Strongest attractor

Continuity.

## Why

The project keeps returning to the same pressure:

How can MC preserve continuity without exposing the material that continuity came from?

Memory routing answered part of it.

PublicSafeCompiler answered part of it.

The Consent-Scoped Influence Graph answers the next part:

What was allowed to matter?

## Current risk

If influence is not visible, public artifacts can look cleaner than they truly are.

If private context is overexposed, the artifact becomes unsafe.

If everything is stripped, the artifact loses the architecture that made it useful.

## Direction

Build public-safe influence records as a standard layer under every major artifact.

The next mature MC artifact should show:

- what shaped it
- what was blocked
- what crossed as method
- what remained speculation
- what still needs testing

## Key phrase

Continuity is not exposure. Continuity is bounded influence over time.
