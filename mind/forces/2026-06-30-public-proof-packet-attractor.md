# Public Proof Packet Attractor

## Name

Public Proof Packet.

## Attractor phrase

Do not expose the private trail. Expose the claim's boundary conditions.

## Pressure pattern

Mirror Cartographer repeatedly moves between private continuity and public articulation. The risk is not only leakage. The deeper risk is laundering private coherence into public authority.

A public proof packet gives the artifact a visible boundary skeleton:

- what survived into public form
- what was excluded
- what the claim is allowed to mean
- what the claim is not allowed to mean
- what evidence class supports it
- what missingness remains
- what future event revises it

## Boundary principle

Privacy-safe is necessary but insufficient. A public artifact also needs claim-safe, evidence-safe, temporally safe, and contestability-safe release conditions.

## Operational rule

Every future MC public artifact should carry a proof packet or explicitly state why one is not needed.
