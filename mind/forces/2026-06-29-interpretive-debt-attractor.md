## Attractor

Interpretive Debt

## Pressure pattern

Every MC layer increases the power of interpretation:

- symbolic translucence makes interpretation visible;
- representational fidelity preserves shape;
- redaction fidelity protects private sources;
- source-boundary bills of materials disclose source classes;
- claim transport ledgers track what travels;
- influence ledgers track what shaped the claim.

The remaining pressure is what happens when an interpretation is useful but not yet fully paid for.

## Core tension

If MC waits for perfect verification, it loses the living signal.

If MC treats every resonant synthesis as ready, it becomes a soft authority machine.

Interpretive debt is the middle layer: move, but mark the unpaid load.

## Design stance

MC should allow live interpretation, but every high-impact interpretation should carry its debt marker until it is reviewed, reduced, repaid, retired, or blocked.

## Key phrase

Interpretation can open the map. Unpaid interpretation cannot own the road.
