## Revision reason
The current boundary stack needs a compact force phrase to prevent claims from drifting into the wrong authority lane.

## Attractor
A claim is a traveler. Its label is not its passport.

## Compression
- Source tells where the claim came from.
- Status tells what kind of claim it is.
- Privacy tells what may be shown.
- Proof lane tells what it may support.
- Routing tells where it may go next.

## Rule
No claim may cross from reflection into proof, from private-derived method into public source, or from repository activity into efficacy without a documented upgrade condition.

## Key phrase
Do not only ask whether a claim is true. Ask where it is allowed to stand.
