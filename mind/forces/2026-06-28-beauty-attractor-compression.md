## Attractor

Beauty

## Force

Beauty is pulling MC toward a sharper product truth:

The system cannot treat aesthetics as decoration.

It also cannot treat aesthetics as proof.

Beauty is a compression force. It can gather scattered signals into a coherent shape. But a coherent shape can overstate its own correctness.

## Current tension

MC needs to be gorgeous enough to make complex inner or conceptual states graspable.

MC also needs to be disciplined enough that the user can reject the artifact, revise it, or see what is unsupported.

## Design consequence

Every powerful aesthetic return should include visible boundary scaffolding.

Not ugly disclaimers.

Not legal dead weight.

Elegant source labels.

Beautiful uncertainty.

A ritualized way to say:

This is what the artifact sees.

This is what it guesses.

This is what it does not know.

This is where you can disagree.

## Risk if ignored

MC becomes persuasive rather than orienting.

The artifact feels true because it is beautiful.

That is not acceptable.

## Risk if overcorrected

MC becomes safe but dead.

The artifact becomes a compliance memo and loses the symbolic force that makes MC distinct.

## Working resolution

Keep the beauty.

Expose the boundary.

Let the artifact glow with its uncertainty visible.
