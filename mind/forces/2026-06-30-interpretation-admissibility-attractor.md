# Attractor: Interpretation Admissibility

## Name

Interpretation Admissibility

## Compression

Meaning may be invited. It is not automatically admitted.

## System pressure

Mirror Cartographer produces value by making symbol, body language, metaphor, recurrence, and emotional pattern visible. That same strength creates risk when interpretation moves too easily from felt coherence into authority.

The attractor is a gate: not every available meaning gets to enter the output.

## Boundary sentence

A symbolic interpretation must earn its rendering status.

## Public-safe form

Publish:

- the admission classes;
- the scorecard;
- the schema;
- the fixture suite;
- the product requirements;
- the missingness record;
- the revision reason.

Do not publish:

- private source examples;
- raw transcripts;
- personal/household/health/animal-care/financial/location/relationship/credential details;
- domain-sensitive interpretation as authority.

## Relation to prior mind layers

- Boundary Stack says every boundary is layered.
- Public Proof Packet says proof must travel without the private path.
- Release Scope says redaction alone does not control the claim that remains.
- Revision Provenance says correction must have custody without exposing private correction.
- Evidence Before Belief says evidence enters before belief.
- Interpretation Admissibility says interpretation itself needs an entry gate.

## Design implication

The renderer should not ask only, "What can the model say?"

It should ask, "What status has this interpretation earned?"

## Failure mode if absent

- Resonance becomes proof.
- Mythopoetic tone becomes authority.
- Private context leaks through abstraction.
- Consequential-domain suggestions appear as symbolic certainty.
- User disagreement is treated as resistance instead of signal.

## Success condition

A user can receive a meaningful reflection while still knowing exactly what kind of claim it is, what is missing, why it was allowed, and how to disagree.
