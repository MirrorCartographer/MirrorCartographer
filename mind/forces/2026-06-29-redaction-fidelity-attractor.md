## Attractor

Redaction Fidelity

## Pull

MC is pulled toward a harder standard than privacy-by-deletion. It must prove that abstraction did not become flattening.

## What this attractor protects

- Private source boundaries.
- Structural meaning.
- Claim humility.
- Public usefulness.
- Reviewer contestability.

## Failure modes

1. Leakage: private facts remain inferable.
2. Flattening: the public artifact becomes generic and loses the actual method.
3. Authority inflation: abstraction sounds more proven than it is.
4. Aesthetic laundering: beautiful language hides weak evidence.
5. Ghost source: private material influences public claims without visible boundary labels.

## Operating principle

A public-safe MC artifact should make the transformation visible without making the source visible.

## Key phrase

The source can stay hidden. The crossing cannot.
