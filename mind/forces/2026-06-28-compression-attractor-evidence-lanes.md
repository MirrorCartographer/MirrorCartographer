## Revision reason
Compression is strongest now because the mind has many components: compiler, packet, risk gate, ViewDiff, operating loop. The next useful move is to compress them into one rule that prevents overreach.

## Force
The current force is lane separation.

MC can hold broad meaning without letting broad meaning become broad proof.

## Pressure
If every pattern can connect to every other pattern, the system becomes beautiful but unverifiable.

If every pattern is isolated, the system becomes accurate but dead.

The design problem is to preserve resonance while forcing evidence to travel through the correct lane.

## Operating phrase
The map may cross domains. The proof cannot.

## Design consequence
All public-safe artifacts should now include an evidence lane before they include a confident claim.
