# Continuity Attractor: Audience Contract

## Attractor

Continuity.

## Force pattern

Mirror Cartographer is no longer missing more expressive language. It is missing a stable rule for changing audiences without changing truth status invisibly.

The strongest force in this pass is the need to bind continuity to audience.

## Compression

Private state does not become public artifact by cleaning.

It becomes public artifact by declaring:

- who it is for,
- what it is allowed to do,
- what it must not do,
- what evidence lane it belongs to,
- what changed,
- what remains unknown,
- who must review it,
- why it can or cannot leave the private workspace.
