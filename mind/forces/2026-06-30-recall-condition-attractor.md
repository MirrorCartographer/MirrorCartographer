# Recall Condition Attractor

## Name

Recall Condition

## Attractor sentence

Relevance opens the door. Conditions decide whether the source may enter.

## Why it matters

Mirror Cartographer is built around long-context reflection, symbolic recurrence, source boundaries, and human-AI interpretation. That makes it vulnerable to a subtle failure: a source feels relevant, so the system lets it shape the output more strongly than it should.

The recall-condition attractor forces the system to pause before interpretation.

## Boundary distinction

- Privacy gate: may this source be exposed?
- Claim gate: what claim can this source support?
- Release gate: what may the artifact become publicly?
- Recall-condition gate: under what conditions may this source influence the present answer?

## Design pressure

The system should prefer a weaker, cleaner public claim over a stronger claim built from unclear recall conditions.

## Product mantra

Do not retrieve and believe. Retrieve, condition, route, then speak.

## Public-safe status

This attractor is public-safe. It contains no raw private source material and no sensitive personal details.
