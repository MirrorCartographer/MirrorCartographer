## Attractor

Continuity.

## Pull

The mind keeps returning to the same unresolved shape:

How can a private meaning-state become useful to another audience without either exposing the private source or severing the source lineage?

## New compression

Continuity is preserved by visible transformation, not by keeping every detail.

## Operating principle

Every meaningful public-safe artifact should be able to answer:

- What private or prior structure shaped this?
- What source category is allowed to be named?
- What claim category is allowed?
- What was removed?
- What was abstracted?
- What was added?
- What became missing?
- Why was the revision made?

## Product implication

Living ViewDiff becomes the continuity instrument: the bridge between personal meaning, professional usefulness, public-safe method, and research-safe aggregation.

## Failure mode

If MC only redacts, it loses meaning.
If MC only preserves meaning, it risks privacy.
If MC only summarizes, it hides transformation.

The continuity attractor requires all three: preservation, protection, and visible transformation.
