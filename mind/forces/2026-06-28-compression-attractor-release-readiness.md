Date: 2026-06-28

## Current force
The repository has accumulated many useful gates. The risk is that the gates become impressive but hard to operate.

## Compression move
Compress the gates into one card:
source -> claim -> privacy -> transformation -> missingness -> lane -> review -> release decision -> next test

## Why this matters
A living workspace needs a door. A door needs a handle. The Release Readiness Card is the handle for public-safe publication.

## Tension
- Too little structure: public artifacts leak context or overclaim.
- Too much structure: the system becomes ceremonial instead of usable.
- Correct structure: one visible release decision that preserves the audit trail.
