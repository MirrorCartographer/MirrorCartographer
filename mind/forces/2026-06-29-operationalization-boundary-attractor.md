# Attractor: Operationalization Boundary

Date: 2026-06-29
Status: public-safe force note

## Name

Operationalization Boundary

## Shape

This attractor appears when symbolic meaning starts to point toward external-world action.

It is the line between:

- noticing and deciding,
- reflection and instruction,
- orientation and authority,
- symbolic fit and practical consequence.

## Why it matters

Mirror Cartographer is strongest when it helps users preserve meaning without pretending that meaning alone proves what should happen next.

Without this boundary, a beautiful reflection can become a hidden command.

## Boundary sentence

Meaning can orient action. It cannot smuggle itself into authority.
