## Revision reason

## Force
Discovery is strongest because the repository no longer needs another name for the same boundary. It needs a visible test object.

The Reviewable Lane Scorecard is that object.

It asks:
- Can a reader see the source?
- Can a reader see the claim type?
- Can a reader see what was removed or transformed?
- Can a reader see why the claim is allowed to travel?
- Can a reader see who has authority to review it?

## Key pressure
