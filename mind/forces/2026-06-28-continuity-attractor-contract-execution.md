## Force pattern

Declaration without execution becomes decoration.

Execution without ledger becomes unverifiable.

Ledger without revision becomes archive rot.

Revision without missingness becomes quiet drift.

## Public-safe design implication

Every public artifact should be able to answer:

- What contract governed this output?
- What changed from source to release?
- What claim ceiling constrained it?
- What privacy ceiling constrained it?
- Who or what reviewed it?
- Why was it released?
- What would trigger revision?
