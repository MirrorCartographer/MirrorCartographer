## Attractor

Revision Provenance

## Core phrase

**Do not expose the private correction. Expose that correction had custody.**

## Meaning

MC should not treat a public claim as stable just because it is safely worded once. Claims move. They are narrowed, downgraded, superseded, corrected, contested, and retired.

The public artifact should show the movement without revealing protected sources.

## Pressure map

- Claim boundary answers: what kind of claim is this?
- Source boundary answers: where can this claim point?
- Privacy boundary answers: what must not travel?
- Temporal boundary answers: is this still current?
- Revision boundary answers: why did this claim change?

## Product implication

Add a revision receipt to public-safe claims, especially when private context influences public architecture without becoming publishable evidence.
