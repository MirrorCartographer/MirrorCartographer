# Attractor: Boundary Regression

## Source Status
Public-safe synthesis from MC architecture and current AI memory-risk research.

## Claim Status
Interpretive product-strategy note.

## Privacy Status
Public-safe. No private source material.

## Missingness
Does not inspect a complete codebase or CI pipeline.

## Revision Reason
Added after prior gates and ledgers exposed a remaining need: retesting the boundary after every future change.

## Attractor
**Boundary Regression**

## Pattern
A system can build careful privacy and authority boundaries, then lose them through ordinary iteration. The danger is not only initial leakage. The danger is later drift: one new artifact, one enthusiastic phrase, one copied context fragment, one unmarked inference, one public release too far.

## MC Implication
Mirror Cartographer should treat public safety as a living invariant. Every public artifact should be retested for source status, claim status, privacy status, missingness, revision reason, and representational fidelity.

## Design Pressure
The system must hold two constraints at once:

1. Do not expose the source.
2. Do not erase the shape of what the source taught the architecture.

Boundary regression testing is the repeatable proof that both constraints still hold.

## Key Phrase
**A boundary that is not retested becomes decoration.**
