## Core phrase

A private pattern may shape the architecture without entering the archive.

## Design pressure

Mirror Cartographer repeatedly encounters material that is architecturally informative but not publishable. The error would be to treat privacy as a removal step only. The stronger design is to treat privacy as a transformation gate.

A private source can produce a public-safe method only after it passes through abstraction, boundary labels, missingness, and claim weakening.
