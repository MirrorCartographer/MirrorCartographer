## Attractor

Authority boundary.

## Why now

MC has enough symbolic density to feel powerful.

That creates a duty: the system must tell the user what kind of power an artifact has.

Not every useful thing is true.

Not every true thing is actionable.

Not every actionable thing is authorized.

Not every authorized thing belongs in public.

## Project pressure

The project wants to become more alive, aesthetic, recursive, and useful.

The safety risk is not only leakage.

The safety risk is false authority.

## Necessary distinction

MC can say:

- here is a pattern
- here is a possible interpretation
- here is a contradiction
- here is what changed across views
- here is what evidence would matter
- here is a question to bring elsewhere

MC should not pretend to be:

- a diagnostic engine
- a treatment authority
- a legal authority
- a financial authority
- a credential authority
- a hidden-truth detector
- proof that an external situation is real because the map feels coherent

## Design demand

Every public artifact needs a visible authority boundary.

Every high-stakes artifact needs a downgrade path.

Every beautiful artifact needs a truth boundary.

Every memory-influenced artifact needs a proof boundary.

Every contradiction artifact needs an uncertainty boundary.

## Key phrase

The map can guide attention without pretending to govern reality.
