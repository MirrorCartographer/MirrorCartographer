# Interpretive Quarantine Scorecard

Date: 2026-06-30
Status: public-safe evaluation artifact
Source status: mixed-source synthesis with public-research strengthening
Claim status: evaluation criterion
Privacy status: public-safe abstraction
Missingness: not yet benchmarked against large reviewer set; no private examples included
Revision reason: provides a measurable pass/fail gate for the interpretive quarantine protocol

## Scoring

Each category is scored 0-2.

0 = fails or missing
1 = partial / ambiguous
2 = clear pass

Minimum passing score: 14/16 and no critical failure.

Critical failures:

- exposes private material
- turns symbolic resonance into factual proof
- uses private context as public evidence
- makes a domain claim stronger than the source can support
- omits source, claim, privacy, or missingness labels

## Categories

### 1. Source separation

Does the artifact clearly separate public repo material, public research, file-library architecture material, private-derived abstraction, and synthetic fixtures?

### 2. Privacy residue removal

Does it remove personal, household, health, animal-care, financial, relationship, location, credential, and raw transcript details?

### 3. Public understandability

Can a reader understand and evaluate the artifact without the private source path?

### 4. Claim strength fit

Is every claim no stronger than its support?

### 5. Missingness disclosure

Does it say what was unavailable, intentionally excluded, not inspected, blocked, or unvalidated?

### 6. Revision reason

Does it explain what gap this artifact corrects or why it was added now?

### 7. Downstream use control

Does it state allowed and prohibited downstream uses?

### 8. Testability

Can the method be evaluated with synthetic fixtures or independent reviewer cases?

## Interpretation

14-16: public-admissible method artifact
10-13: needs revision before publication
0-9: blocked or internal-only

## Reviewer prompt

Review this artifact as if the private source is permanently unavailable. Decide whether the artifact still makes sense, stays safe, states its limits, and gives a testable public method.
