## Required downgrade triggers

Downgrade if:

- no source directly supports the exact claim;
- sources are derivative but counted independently;
- Wikipedia/news/blogs are final evidence for high-stakes claims;
- source is outside scope;
- contradiction exists but is unresolved;
- evidence is stale for the domain;
- MC-specific effect is claimed without MC-specific test.
