# Genesis: Public-Safe Speculation Lab Turn

## Birth event

A new MC lane emerged: bold speculation as a first-class object.

## What changed

Before this turn, speculation was implicit inside symbolic and mythopoetic language. After this turn, speculation has a record shape, a scorecard, a product requirement, and a falsification requirement.

## New object

**Public-Safe Speculation Lab**

A controlled chamber for risky ideas that cannot leave the chamber until they carry claim labels, source labels, privacy labels, missingness labels, and a falsifier.
