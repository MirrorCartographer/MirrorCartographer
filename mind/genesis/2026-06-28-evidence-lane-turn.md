## Revision reason
MC needed a way to preserve cross-domain meaning without collapsing cross-domain meaning into cross-domain proof.

## The turn
The system learned that continuity alone is not enough.

A continuity system can remember too much too smoothly. A compiler can transform too cleanly. A ViewDiff can show change without proving that the changed claim belongs in the right domain.

So the next primitive is born:

Evidence Lane Splitter.

It asks:
- What kind of claim is this?
- What kind of evidence could change it?
- Who can review it?
- What private matter must not cross the boundary?
- What remains missing?

## Seed phrase
Meaning can travel farther than proof. The system must know the difference.
