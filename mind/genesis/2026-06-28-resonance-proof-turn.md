## What changed

The project had already built strong lanes for memory routing, public-safe compilation, ViewDiff discovery, contradiction, beauty boundaries, and authority limits.

This turn adds a necessary interior guard:

Resonance is not proof.

## Why it matters

MC depends on resonance feedback.

Without resonance, the map cannot adapt to the user's lived symbolic field.

But if resonance becomes evidence, the system can overfit to relief, intensity, beauty, and agreement.

That creates false authority.

## New principle

Resonance should update symbolic weights.

It should not increase factual confidence unless supported by source status, external evidence, or repeated bounded confirmation.

## The turn

The map learned to listen without believing everything it heard.

## Key phrase

The feeling of fit is a signal. It is not a verdict.
