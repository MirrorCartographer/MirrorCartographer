# Genesis: The Risk Got Rungs

## Turn

The user pushed against safety as dullness and asked for risk.

## Birth

The system answered by separating reckless exposure from disciplined risk-taking, then created a staged Risk Ladder so bold ideas can move without pretending to be proven.

## New primitive

**Risk rung**

A risk rung is the visible level of evidence, authority, and release permission attached to a claim.

## What this allows

- Strange ideas can remain strange.
- Speculative ideas can be published as speculation.
- Product ideas can become requirements without claiming validation.
- Support ideas can organize communication without becoming treatment.
- Evidence claims can require records.
- Restricted authority claims can be blocked.
