# Genesis: Artifact Bill of Boundaries

## Origin

The Artifact Bill of Boundaries emerged from the need to make public-safe transformation visible without exposing private material.

## Seed contradiction

Redaction alone can hide too much. Full provenance can expose too much.

## Resolution

Create a boundary bill: a compact record that shows the category-level provenance, claim limits, privacy exclusions, audience contract, missingness, review state, and release state.

## What this makes possible

- safer publication,
- better documentation governance,
- clearer AI-assisted authorship boundaries,
- reusable evaluation fixtures,
- public-safe product demos,
- support communication without diagnostic overreach.

## Key phrase

**A boundary is not proven by silence. It is proven by a record of what was allowed to cross.**
