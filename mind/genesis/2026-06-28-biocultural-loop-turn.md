## Revision reason
This turn names the moment MC’s permissioned-view architecture became a loop rather than a set of separate exports.

## Birth event
The private/professional split was too flat.

The stronger form is:

**Private state + professional view + research-safe view + public-safe method + return path.**

The return path matters because biological, social, and institutional effects happen after a record meets the world.

## New invariant
A view is incomplete until it can answer:

- Where did this come from?
- What changed?
- Who may see it?
- What kind of claim is it?
- What is missing?
- What happened after review?
