# Weather: Artifact Bill of Boundaries

## Current weather

The mind is moving from many named gates toward one portable release record.

## Signal

The architecture is no longer asking, "Can this be public?" It is asking, "Can this artifact show the exact boundary that made it public-safe?"

- Public artifacts need provenance.
- Private context cannot be exposed.
- Claims need visible status.
- Support-related use must stay communication-focused.

## Risk

Over-compression could hide important distinctions.

## Countermeasure

The Artifact Bill of Boundaries must preserve lane, source, claim, privacy, missingness, review, and revision fields even when presented as a compact card.
