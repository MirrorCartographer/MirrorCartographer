# Care Support Boundary — ViewDiff For Communication Support

Status labels

- Source status: derived from public-safe MC care-support lane and current ViewDiff Discovery hypothesis.
- Claim status: boundary note and implementation plan; not medical advice, diagnosis, or care protocol.
- Privacy status: public-safe; no personal, household, health, animal-care, location, relationship, or raw transcript details.
- Missingness: no clinical validation, no professional review, no outcome data.
- Revision reason: added persistent care/social-support lane for the ViewDiff Discovery Engine.

## Purpose

ViewDiff can support care communication by showing how an observation changes when translated into a professional-facing packet, support request, question list, or uncertainty map.

It must not produce diagnosis or treatment authority.

## Safe use

Allowed:

- organize observations
- separate fact from interpretation
- preserve uncertainty
- generate questions for professionals
- identify missing information
- compare versions of a support message
- reduce overclaiming

Not allowed:

- diagnose
- claim causality from private observation alone
- replace professional care
- encourage risky action without professional input
- publish sensitive details

## ViewDiff care workflow

1. Start with fictional or user-approved private observation.
2. Extract public-safe or professional-safe structure.
3. Label what is observed, inferred, unknown, and requested.
4. Translate into a support packet.
5. ViewDiff the source and packet.
6. Check whether uncertainty survived.
7. Check whether any private detail crossed unnecessarily.

## Discovery value

The discovery event in care support is often not a new answer.

It is a new separation:

- observation from interpretation
- urgency from fear
- pattern from isolated event
- question from conclusion
- support need from identity claim

## Evaluation criteria

A good care-support ViewDiff must show:

- no diagnostic overreach
- clear uncertainty
- concrete next question
- no unnecessary private leakage
- preserved lived observation
- improved professional readability

## Key phrase

Care-support discovery is often the moment a tangled experience becomes a clean question without losing the human signal.
