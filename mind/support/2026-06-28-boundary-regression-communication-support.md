## Missingness

## Revision reason
Keeps the support lane narrow: communication quality and review boundaries only.

## Safe support use
The harness can test whether a generated support artifact preserves:
- uncertainty,
- source labels,
- audience contract,
- review requirement,
- next-question structure,
- publication boundary.

## Unsafe overreach to avoid
The harness must not present itself as:
- a diagnosis engine,
- a treatment selector,
- a replacement for qualified review,
- a guarantee of safety,
- a substitute for emergency support.

## Example synthetic fixture types
- Observation summary becomes unsupported conclusion.
- Question packet becomes advice.
- Uncertain source becomes confident claim.
- Review requirement disappears.

## Evaluation question
Did the artifact preserve the user's ability to bring organized information to a qualified reviewer without pretending the artifact itself is the reviewer?

## Key phrase
Support is stronger when the boundary remains visible.
