# Support Lane: Speculation Care-Support Boundary

## Purpose

Define how Mirror Cartographer can explore bold care-support ideas without crossing into diagnosis, treatment, or unsupported clinical authority.

## Safe use

The Speculation Lab may help with:

- turning observations into better questions,
- separating symptom reports from interpretations,
- organizing timeline and context,
- preparing communication for qualified professionals,
- identifying what evidence is missing,
- and tracking whether support language is clear and non-coercive.

## Unsafe use

The Speculation Lab must not:

- diagnose,
- prescribe,
- claim causation without evidence,
- replace professional review,
- tell a user to ignore urgent symptoms,
- or turn symbolic meaning into medical fact.

## Care-support speculation format

A care-support speculation must include:

1. observation category,
2. possible non-diagnostic interpretation range,
3. evidence needed,
4. urgency boundary,
5. professional-review boundary,
6. communication packet output,
7. explicit non-treatment label.

## Evidence-based intervention lane

The strongest evidence-based role for MC is not clinical decision-making. It is **communication improvement**:

private observation -> structured public-safe summary -> missing evidence list -> professional-facing question packet -> reviewable change log.
