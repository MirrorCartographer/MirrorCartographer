## Offer

Public-Safe AI Artifact Audit

## Plain description

A review service for people or teams using AI to create public-facing research, wellness-adjacent, artistic, educational, or grant/application materials.

The audit checks whether the artifact is clear, source-labeled, privacy-safe, appropriately bounded, and not making unsupported claims.

## Buyer pain

People are using AI to make serious artifacts, but many outputs have hidden risks:

- unclear source lineage
- confident wording without evidence
- private context leakage
- accidental clinical authority claims
- persuasive aesthetics that feel true but are not tested
- missing revision history
- vague AI-generated language that cannot survive review

## Deliverable

For one artifact, deliver:

- source-status review
- claim-status review
- privacy-risk review
- missingness review
- revision-label review
- public-safe rewrite guidance
- release-readiness score
- next-test recommendation
