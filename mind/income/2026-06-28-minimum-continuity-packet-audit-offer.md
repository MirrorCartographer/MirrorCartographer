# Income Lane: Minimum Continuity Packet Audit

Date: 2026-06-28
Attractor: Compression
Privacy status: public-safe
Claim status: realistic opportunity hypothesis, not guaranteed income

## Offer
A compact audit for teams using AI to summarize, transform, or share human-generated information.

## Buyer problem
Teams are adopting AI faster than they can explain what changed between original input and output. This creates risk around trust, provenance, training, review, compliance, and user confidence.

## Public-safe product shape
Deliver a short report that maps one workflow into:

- source labels
- claim labels
- privacy labels
- missingness labels
- revision reasons
- ViewDiff notes
- risk gates
- recommended UI language
- evaluation checklist

## Best-fit customers
- AI literacy trainers
- small healthcare-adjacent documentation teams
- social-care documentation pilots
- coaching or reflection products
- research teams using LLMs in literature notes
- product teams adding AI summaries
- nonprofits adopting AI tools

## Boundaries
This is not legal, clinical, security, or compliance certification. It is a structured product/research audit for clarity, safety language, and workflow design.

## Why now
Fresh public signals show demand for AI literacy, workforce training, provenance-aware AI use, and safer AI documentation. Reporting on health and social-care AI documentation errors also shows the cost of unreviewed AI summaries.

## Minimum deliverable
A 5-page PDF or markdown packet:

1. Workflow snapshot
2. Claim/source/privacy map
3. ViewDiff example
4. Missingness and revision table
5. Practical next-step checklist
