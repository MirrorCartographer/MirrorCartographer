# Income Lane: Speculative Hypothesis Hardening

## Offer

**Speculative Hypothesis Hardening** is a paid Mirror Cartographer-adjacent service that takes a messy, ambitious, symbolic, or early-stage idea and turns it into:

- a clear hypothesis,
- a claim-boundary record,
- a falsification plan,
- a public-safe explanation,
- an evaluation scorecard,
- and a first implementation or research path.

## Why this is realistic

Many founders, artists, researchers, and AI builders have ideas that are too early, too weird, or too cross-disciplinary for normal business documents. The pain is not lack of ideas. The pain is conversion:

messy signal -> coherent claim -> testable artifact -> public-safe explanation -> next action.

MC is already structurally suited to that conversion.

## First deliverable package

1. One-page idea map.
2. Strongest-version hypothesis.
3. Boundary bill.
4. Falsifier list.
5. Market/use-case fit note.
6. Evaluation scorecard.
7. Public-safe pitch paragraph.
8. Next three tests.
