## Offer

Aesthetic Claim Audit

A public-safe review service for AI-generated artifacts that are visually, emotionally, or symbolically persuasive.

## Who it helps

- founders using AI to create investor or public-facing materials;
- wellness or coaching creators using AI-generated language and visuals;
- educators creating reflective or emotionally resonant learning artifacts;
- researchers turning complex ideas into public-facing explainers;
- design teams using AI-generated storyboards, decks, or brand systems.

## Problem

AI can make weak claims look polished.

Beautiful structure can hide uncertainty.

The audit checks whether the artifact's aesthetic force is serving clarity or creating false confidence.

## Deliverable

A short report with:

- claim map;
- source boundary notes;
- unsupported claim flags;
- metaphor-versus-evidence separation;
- uncertainty labels;
- missingness list;
- revision plan;
- public-safe rewrite suggestions.

## What makes it MC-native

MC does not only edit the text.

It inspects the transformation:

source → interpretation → aesthetic artifact → reader belief

The audit asks what survived, what got prettier, what got stronger without proof, and what needs a boundary tag.

## Minimum viable demo

Create a fictional AI-generated wellness/brand/research artifact and publish a public-safe audit sample.

## Price hypothesis

- Starter audit: low-cost fixed review.
- Full audit: deeper report with rewritten claim map.
- Team workshop: live review of multiple AI-generated artifacts.

## Risk

This offer must not become generic copyediting.

The differentiator is epistemic aesthetics: checking how beauty changes belief.

## Key phrase

Make it beautiful. Then make it honest enough to survive being beautiful.
