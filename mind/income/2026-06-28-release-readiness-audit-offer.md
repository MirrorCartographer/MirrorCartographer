# Release Readiness Audit Offer

Date: 2026-06-28
Status: public-safe income note
Attractor: Compression

## Offer
A lightweight audit for AI-assisted creators, small teams, coaches, educators, and project builders who need to know whether an AI-generated artifact is safe and clear enough to publish.

## Problem
Many AI outputs sound polished before they are actually source-bounded, claim-bounded, privacy-safe, reviewable, or audience-ready.

## Productized service
Deliver a one-page Release Readiness Card for each artifact:
- What sources were used?
- What claims are being made?
- What private context was transformed or removed?
- What is missing?
- What review is required?
- What can be published now?
- What must stay private?

## Possible package
- 5 artifacts reviewed
- one card per artifact
- one summary index
- one rewrite recommendation list
- no raw private-data handling in public deliverables

## Buyer
- independent AI builders
- educators creating AI-assisted materials
- small organizations using AI for public communications
- coaches or facilitators who need publication hygiene
- artists and symbolic-system builders who need boundary labeling

## Differentiator
This is not generic proofreading. It audits transformation lineage: how private or messy input became a public-facing artifact.

## Claim status
Realistic income hypothesis, not a guarantee.

## Privacy status
Public-safe. No private client examples included.
