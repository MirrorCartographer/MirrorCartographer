# Income Lane: Risk Ladder Audit Offer

## Offer

A public-safe **AI Artifact Risk Ladder Audit** for teams that use AI to generate public-facing documents, research pages, support content, education material, marketing claims, or internal-to-external summaries.

## Buyer pain

Many organizations publish AI-assisted artifacts without a clear distinction between:

- metaphor,
- hypothesis,
- requirement,
- evidence,
- support language,
- market claim,
- and authority claim.

This creates risk: overclaiming, privacy leakage, unsupported validation language, unclear reviewer status, and loss of trust.

## Deliverable

For each artifact, provide:

1. risk ladder level,
2. claim status,
3. source status,
4. privacy status,
5. reviewer status,
6. missingness note,
7. required evidence to climb,
8. suggested safer release form,
9. blocked phrases or unsupported claims,
10. public-safe rewrite path.

## Realistic first market

Small teams that already produce public AI-assisted content but lack formal review operations:

- AI startups,
- research labs,
- digital-health education teams,
- nonprofit program teams,
- creator-led educational products,
- knowledge-management consultants,
- compliance-light but reputation-sensitive software companies.

## Why this is sellable

The buyer does not need to adopt all of Mirror Cartographer. They can buy a narrow artifact review:

> "Tell us which claims are safe to publish, which are only hypotheses, which need evidence, and which need qualified review."

## Evidence boundary

This is a product hypothesis, not proven market demand. It should be tested with 3-5 sample audits, public-safe before/after examples, and outreach to teams that publish AI-assisted documentation.

## Minimum viable paid service

A fixed-scope review of one public artifact or landing page:

- identify claim types,
- assign risk ladder levels,
- mark unsafe escalations,
- produce a public-safe release checklist,
- deliver a rewrite map.

## Key phrase

**Sell the ladder before selling the whole map.**

## Source status

Public-safe product synthesis from current GitHub mind and fresh AI provenance / safety research.

## Claim status

Income hypothesis and service-design draft.

## Privacy status

Public-safe; no personal, financial, household, health, animal-care, location, relationship, credential, or raw transcript content included.

## Missingness

No customer interviews, pricing tests, or buyer commitments yet. Needs sample audits and market contact list.
