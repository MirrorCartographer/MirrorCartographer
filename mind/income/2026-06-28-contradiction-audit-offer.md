# Income Lane — Contradiction Audit Offer

Status labels

- Source status: derived from MC product direction and public research on AI reliance, provenance, and uncertainty.
- Claim status: practical offer hypothesis, not proof of demand.
- Privacy status: public-safe.
- Missingness: no pricing test, no landing page, no customer interviews.
- Revision reason: created to keep the practical income lane attached to the strongest current attractor.

## Offer

A `Contradiction Audit` reviews an AI-generated artifact and identifies where important tensions were hidden, flattened, or made falsely confident.

## Buyer types

- AI product teams
- researchers using AI-assisted writing
- founders preparing investor or grant materials
- educators evaluating AI-generated student support content
- health-adjacent or care-adjacent teams that need strict non-authority boundaries

## Deliverable

A short report with:

- contradiction map
- source-boundary notes
- claim-status labels
- missingness register
- risk notes
- revision recommendations
- public-safe rewrite or synthetic demo

## Why MC is suited

MC already treats meaning as a structured object rather than plain prose.

That makes it useful for finding where prose has become too smooth.

## First demo

Use a synthetic AI-generated product claim and show:

1. the original artifact,
2. the contradiction map,
3. the safer revised artifact,
4. the remaining missingness.

## Key phrase

The audit sells the thing most AI writing hides: the tension that matters.
