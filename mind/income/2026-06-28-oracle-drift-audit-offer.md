# Oracle Drift Audit Offer

## Source status
Public-safe income-lane artifact derived from Mirror Cartographer's fixture, runner, oracle, and drift architecture.

## Claim status
Market hypothesis. Not validated by customer discovery.

## Privacy status
Public-safe. Contains no private customer, personal, health, animal-care, household, financial, credential, location, relationship, or transcript detail.

## Missingness
Needs buyer interviews, pricing tests, example deliverables, and proof that teams will pay for this category.

## Revision reason
The prior offer focused on fixture/oracle testing. This revision adds expectation-versioning as a distinct audit value.

## Offer
AI Output Boundary Regression + Oracle Drift Audit

## Plain-language promise
We test whether public-facing AI outputs preserve provenance, privacy boundaries, claim boundaries, evidence lanes, and audience contracts across synthetic cases. When a test fails, we document whether the output failed or the expected-output oracle needs revision.

## Best-fit buyers
- AI teams publishing documentation or summaries
- healthcare software teams using AI drafts under human review
- legal or compliance documentation workflows
- research organizations using AI-assisted synthesis
- enterprise knowledge-management teams

## Deliverables
1. Synthetic fixture pack
2. Oracle records for expected boundary behavior
3. Runner result summary
4. Drift ledger for mismatches
5. Release-readiness recommendations
6. Regression plan

## Why this is concrete
This avoids selling vague symbolic value. The buyer receives a visible artifact set:
- what was tested,
- what was expected,
- what failed,
- whether the expectation changed,
- what can be released,
- what must be reviewed or blocked.

## Boundary
This offer does not certify legal compliance, medical safety, or regulatory approval. It is a documentation-governance and release-readiness support service.

## First small version
A fixed-price review of five synthetic fixtures and five public-facing AI output examples, producing a short boundary regression report and drift ledger.
