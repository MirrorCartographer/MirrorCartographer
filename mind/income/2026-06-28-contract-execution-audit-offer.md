# Contract Execution Audit Offer

## Summary

A practical income-facing wedge for Mirror Cartographer adjacent work: audit whether an AI-assisted artifact obeyed its declared audience, claim, privacy, evidence, and review boundaries.

## Source status

Public-safe synthesis from the Mirror Cartographer repository and public research on AI provenance, inspectable AI workflows, AI transparency, and health documentation governance.

## Claim status

Commercial hypothesis. Not proof of demand, income, legal compliance, or product-market fit.

## Privacy status

Public-safe. No private client, personal, household, health, animal-care, financial, relationship, credential, location, or transcript details.

## Offer name

Contract Execution Audit for AI-Assisted Public Artifacts

## Buyer problem

Organizations increasingly use AI to turn internal notes, research, conversations, support material, or expert knowledge into public-facing artifacts.

The risk is not only that private material leaks. The risk is that the public artifact silently changes claim level, audience, certainty, consent boundary, or evidentiary meaning.

## Deliverable

For one artifact:

1. Audience Contract.
2. Evidence Lane label.
3. Claim Ceiling label.
4. Privacy Ceiling label.
5. ViewDiff summary.
6. Contract Execution Ledger.
7. Release Readiness decision.
8. Public-safe revision note.

## Concrete buyer categories

- small AI product teams,
- research labs publishing AI-assisted explainers,
- solo consultants using AI to repurpose sensitive notes,
- patient-support or care-navigation startups,
- legal-adjacent documentation teams,
- nonprofit knowledge teams,
- creators with private archives being transformed into public products.

## Pricing hypothesis

Pilot audit: low fixed fee for one artifact and one revised version.

Expanded package: monthly review of a small number of public artifacts.

## Why this is adjacent to MC

Mirror Cartographer already focuses on transformation boundaries: symbol versus evidence, private reflection versus public claim, and continuity without omniscience.

The audit offer abstracts that architecture into a concrete service that does not require selling the full symbolic reflection system first.

## Evaluation criteria

The offer is viable only if a buyer can understand the value in one sentence:

Show what changed between the private source and the public artifact, and prove the final artifact stayed inside its declared boundary.
