# Gate Router Audit Offer

## Summary
A realistic income wedge for Mirror Cartographer is a documentation-readiness audit that turns messy high-context material into a routed publication decision.

## Offer Name
Gate Router Audit.

## Buyer Problem
Teams using AI to transform internal notes, research, interviews, support logs, clinical-adjacent summaries, or governance records need to know what can safely become public, what requires review, and what should remain private.

## Public-Safe Deliverable
For each artifact, deliver:

- audience contract
- source status
- claim status
- privacy status
- evidence lane
- missingness note
- transformation summary
- router state
- next action
- revision reason

## Router-Based Output
Each artifact receives one of:

- publishable
- revise
- narrow
- abstract
- review
- hold_private
- discard

## Why This Is More Sellable Than Broad MC Language
"Symbolic continuity infrastructure" is powerful but abstract. "We classify whether this artifact is publishable, needs revision, requires review, should be abstracted, or must stay private" is operational and easier to buy.

## Initial Customer Segments
- AI documentation teams
- governance and compliance teams
- research organizations
- healthcare software teams working on non-diagnostic documentation support
- creator/research teams publishing sensitive stories or interviews
- enterprise knowledge-management teams

## Proof Artifact
Create a fictional fixture pack with 10 synthetic source snippets and route each through the Gate-to-Action Router. No real personal or sensitive source material is needed.

## Pricing Hypothesis
Start as a small fixed-scope audit:

- 10 artifacts
- one routing table
- one risk summary
- one recommended publication plan

This can later become a software feature.

## Source Status
GitHub-derived and architecture-derived from current MC gate map, compiler, scorecard, and release-readiness artifacts.

## Claim Status
Business hypothesis, not validated market evidence.

## Privacy Status
Public-safe. No client, personal, health, animal-care, financial, credential, location, relationship, or transcript details.

## Missingness
No customer discovery, pricing tests, or paid pilot data yet.

## Revision Reason
Translate the new Gate-to-Action Router into a concrete income lane.
