## Buyer types
- AI literacy trainers
- small clinics or care-coordination teams needing documentation hygiene education

## Pain being solved
Many people use AI to summarize sensitive or complex material, but they cannot show:

- what changed from source to summary;
- what was omitted;
- what is evidence versus interpretation;
- what is safe to share;
- what needs human review;
- what should never be published.

MC’s income wedge is to package this as a visible, beautiful, low-risk training artifact.

## Demo format
A 45- to 90-minute workshop or downloadable demo kit using fictional records.

Components:
1. one private fictional record;
2. five permissioned views;
3. a Living ViewDiff ledger;
4. a ClaimBoundaryCompiler pass;
5. a BioculturalViewLoop pass;
6. a final public-safe publication decision.

## Validation test
A buyer should be able to answer after the demo:

1. What changed between the private source and public version?
2. Which claims are observations, hypotheses, or reviewed actions?
3. Which data should not leave the private layer?
4. What is still missing?
5. What revision was made and why?
