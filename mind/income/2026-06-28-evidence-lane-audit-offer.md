## Offer name
Evidence Lane Audit

## One-line description
A fixed-scope review that separates AI-assisted claims into evidence lanes so teams can see what is sourced, inferred, speculative, private, missing, or ready to publish.

## Likely buyers
- AI literacy trainers
- small research teams
- coaches or consultants using AI summaries
- founders writing public positioning from private context
- educators teaching responsible AI use
- product teams converting interviews or reflection data into public artifacts

## Buyer problem
AI makes polished summaries that blur the boundary between observation, interpretation, hypothesis, evidence, and publishable claim.

## Deliverables
1. Claim inventory
2. Evidence-lane table
3. Privacy and reidentification risk notes
4. Missingness register
5. Blocked-claim list
6. ViewDiff of before/after summary
7. One-page revision plan

## Starter package
Scope: 3 to 5 artifacts or up to 10 pages of AI-assisted text.
Output: one annotated audit and one clean corrected summary.

## Why Mirror Cartographer fits
MC already treats continuity, evidence, privacy, and transformation as first-class objects. This offer turns that architecture into a small paid service before a full product exists.

## Validation steps
- Create one fictional sample audit.
- Test whether a non-MC reader can understand it in under five minutes.
- Ask whether they would use it before publishing AI-assisted work.
- Measure confusion reduction, not aesthetic approval.

## Claim boundary
This is not legal compliance advice, clinical review, financial advice, or academic integrity certification. It is a publication-hygiene and AI-literacy audit.
