# Audience Contract Audit Offer

## Offer

Mirror Cartographer can be positioned as a public-safe transformation-audit service for teams that need to convert sensitive, complex, or AI-mediated material into audience-specific outputs without losing source boundaries.

## Practical buyer problem

Organizations increasingly need to publish, summarize, teach, or operationalize AI-assisted material. The risk is not only whether the material is true. The risk is whether it is being shown to the wrong audience at the wrong claim level with missing privacy, consent, evidence, or revision labels.

## Service wedge

Create an Audience Contract Ledger for one existing artifact set.

Deliverable:

- audience map,
- allowed and forbidden purpose list,
- claim ceiling,
- privacy ceiling,
- evidence lane,
- transformation log,
- release decision,
- missingness register,
- revision note,
- public-safe rewrite recommendations.

## Possible first customers

- AI literacy programs,
- small AI product teams,
- research groups preparing public summaries,
- healthcare-adjacent documentation teams that need non-clinical communication boundaries,
- creators or educators using AI-assisted material,
- knowledge-management teams converting internal notes into public documentation.

## What not to sell

- Diagnosis,
- treatment planning,
- clinical validation,
- legal compliance guarantee,
- privacy-law certification,
- claims that MC proves truth or causality.
