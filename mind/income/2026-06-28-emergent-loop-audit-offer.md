## Revision reason
Earlier offers focused on individual components. This version packages the full emergent operating loop as a practical service.

## Offer name
**AI Transformation Boundary Audit**

## Buyer problem
Teams are using AI to summarize, rewrite, classify, and publish material, but often cannot show:
- what the source was;
- what changed;
- what was inferred;
- what was removed;
- what remains uncertain;
- who reviewed it;
- whether the final artifact overstates authority.

## MC-shaped solution
Run one artifact through the emergent operating loop:

source units → claim labels → privacy labels → compiled output → ViewDiff ledger → risk gate → revision note

## Deliverable
A short audit packet with:
1. source/claim/privacy table;
2. transformation ledger;
3. missingness list;
4. overclaim warnings;
5. public-safe rewrite suggestions;
6. next-test checklist.

## Realistic first customers
- small AI product teams;
- solo consultants using AI-generated reports;
- educators designing AI literacy assignments;
- nonprofits producing sensitive summaries;
- creators publishing AI-assisted research notes.

## Boundary
This is not compliance certification, legal advice, therapy, medicine, diagnosis, or security assurance. It is a communication/provenance audit for public-facing or semi-public AI-assisted artifacts.

## First proof artifact to build
A fictional before/after audit card showing a messy AI-generated summary turned into a boundary-labeled public-safe artifact.
