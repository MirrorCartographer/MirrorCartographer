## Buyer pain
AI outputs often look finished before they are trustworthy. The weak spots are usually source ambiguity, overclaiming, missing privacy review, hidden transformation, absent reviewer authority, and no next evidence action.

## Fixed-scope package

Deliverable:

- scorecard across 8 dimensions
- blocked-claim list
- privacy/export risk list
