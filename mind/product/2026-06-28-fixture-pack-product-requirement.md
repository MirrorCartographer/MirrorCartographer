# Fixture Pack Product Requirement

## Purpose
Define a public-safe product requirement for Mirror Cartographer: a synthetic fixture pack that demonstrates how private or high-context material can be transformed into public-safe artifacts while preserving source, claim, privacy, missingness, and revision labels.

## Product Object
A downloadable or repository-hosted synthetic fixture pack.

## Required Contents

1. Synthetic source packet.
2. Declared audience.
3. Evidence lane.
4. Claim label.
5. Privacy label.
6. Missingness note.
7. Public-safe transformed output.
8. ViewDiff summary.
9. Router state.
10. Revision reason.

## Public Demo Boundary
The demo must use invented cases only. It must not include private transcripts, personal cases, household details, health narratives, animal-care details, financial details, location details, relationship details, credential details, or private customer material.

## Practical Use
The fixture pack can show whether MC's publication pipeline produces inspectable transformations rather than opaque rewrites.

## Claim Boundary
This is a product requirement and demonstration scaffold. It is not proof of market demand, legal compliance, clinical validity, or security assurance.

## Missingness
No sample fixture pack exists yet. No public demo page exists yet. No user testing has been performed.

## Next Evidence Action
Create three fully synthetic fixture records: one publishable, one abstract-required, and one review-required.
