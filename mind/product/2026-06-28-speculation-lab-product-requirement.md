# Product Requirement: Public-Safe Speculation Lab

## Product intent

Mirror Cartographer should include a mode that lets users explore bold symbolic, architectural, or research hypotheses while keeping source boundaries and claim status visible.

## User need

Users need an interface that does not flatten imaginative reasoning into bland notes, but also does not let vivid language masquerade as proof.

## Core requirement

Every speculative output must show:

1. what the idea claims,
2. what it does not claim,
3. what kind of evidence would change it,
4. what private material was excluded,
5. what the next test or transformation is.

## Interface objects

- Speculation card
- Falsifier field
- Boundary bill field
- Evidence-lane selector
- Release-state selector
- Fixture-test button
- Promote-to-requirement button
- Archive/falsified state

## User-facing states

- Spark
- Hypothesis
- Pattern
- Question
- Test
- Requirement
- Blocked
- Falsified
- Archived

## MVP behavior

When a user enters a strange or ambitious idea, MC should not answer only with encouragement. It should return:

- the strongest version of the idea,
- the safest public abstraction,
- the most obvious ways it could be wrong,
- the first testable version,
- and the correct claim label.

## Commercial wedge

This can become a paid workshop or audit product: **Speculative Hypothesis Hardening**.

Target users:

- founders with messy concept systems,
- researchers forming early hypotheses,
- artists translating symbolic work into public language,
- AI product teams needing visible ideation-to-requirement trails,
- care-support builders needing cautious language boundaries.

## Non-goals

- No diagnosis.
- No treatment recommendation.
- No private transcript publication.
- No proof by metaphor.
- No replacing expert review.

## Source status

Public-safe synthesis from MC architecture and current AI provenance/privacy research.

## Claim status

Product requirement and commercial hypothesis.

## Privacy status

Public-safe. No private examples or raw transcript content.

## Missingness

Needs prototype screen, synthetic fixture pack, and first user-facing demo.

## Revision reason

Move MC from only boundary documentation toward a live interface for high-risk conceptual exploration with visible controls.
