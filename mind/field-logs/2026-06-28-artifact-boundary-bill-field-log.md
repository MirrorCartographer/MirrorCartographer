## What changed

## Why it matters

A public artifact should not make readers guess:

- where it came from,
- what it claims,
- what it excludes,
- who it is for,
- what is missing,
- why it changed,
- whether it is safe to release.

## Public-safe constraint

No raw private context was published. Private material was used only as architectural background, then abstracted into method, schema, and requirements.
