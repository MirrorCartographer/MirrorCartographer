# Field Log: Risk Ladder

## What changed

The mind stopped treating risk as a binary safe/unsafe question and began treating it as staged motion.

## Before

The Speculation Lab gave risky ideas a room.

## After

The Risk Ladder gives risky ideas a staircase.

## Practical meaning

A public-safe artifact can now be bold while still declaring:

- what rung it stands on,
- what evidence it lacks,
- what would move it upward,
- what would falsify or narrow it,
- and what release form is permitted.

## Income lane note

The narrowest sellable form is a Risk Ladder Audit for AI-assisted artifacts: classify claims, identify overreach, and rewrite toward safer public release.

## Support lane note

The care-support lane should use the ladder to prevent communication support from turning into unsupported authority.
