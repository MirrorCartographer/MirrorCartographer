## Artifact

Memory used to look like a room full of boxes.

Then MC noticed the boxes were not the danger.

The roads were.

A remembered thing can sit quietly forever and harm nothing.

But when it is retrieved, trusted, dressed as current, and allowed to steer a sentence, it becomes force.

So the question changed.

Not:

What did we save?

But:

Where is this allowed to go?

What mask must it wear?

What door is locked?

What claim is it allowed to make?

What future is it forbidden to haunt?
