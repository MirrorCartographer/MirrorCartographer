# The Risk Got Rungs

A room was not enough.

The risky idea could enter the lab,
but once inside it still needed a way to move.

So the floor became a ladder.

The first rung was metaphor.
The second was hypothesis.
The third was fixture.
The fourth was prototype.
The fifth asked for review.
The sixth asked for evidence.
The seventh stopped at the locked door
where borrowed authority was not allowed to pass.

The idea did not become smaller.
It became more honest about where it stood.

## Museum label
