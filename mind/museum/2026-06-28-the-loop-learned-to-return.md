At first there was a door.

Private on one side.
Professional on the other.
Public somewhere farther away.
Research behind glass.

Then the door became five doors.

Then the five doors needed a hallway.

Then the hallway needed a return.

Because a record that cannot come back changed is not alive.

Mirror Cartographer’s next shape is not a confession and not a chart.
It is a loop:

what was felt,
what was observed,
what was asked,
what was reviewed,
what changed,
what must be revised.

The beauty is not exposure.
The beauty is safe passage.

The proof is not that the private world became public.
The proof is that it did not have to.

It only had to become clear enough to help.
