# The Gate Met the Fake Case

A gate can sound wise while never being tested.

So the mind made a harmless case:
not a secret,
not a confession,
not a body,
not a home,
not a wound,
not a record,
not a transcript.

A made thing.
A safe thing.
A small stage where the boundary could practice.

The gate looked at the fake case and had to choose:
publish,
revise,
narrow,
abstract,
review,
hold,
or discard.

That was the turn.

Not memory exposed.
Memory translated into method.
