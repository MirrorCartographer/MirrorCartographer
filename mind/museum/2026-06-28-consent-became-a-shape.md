## Artifact
At first there was a room.

Then there were doors.

Then the system learned that a door is not enough.

A door can open too wide.
A door can open for the wrong person.
A door can remember what it should have forgotten.
A door can make a private world look public just because the words got cleaned.

So the door became a prism.

Not yes or no.
Not hidden or exposed.

A shaped passage.

Private meaning entered one side.
A professional question left another.
A public method left another.
A research-safe pattern left another.
Some light did not leave at all.

The value was not the shine.

The value was knowing which beam was allowed to exist.

## Public-safe phrase
**Consent became a shape, and the shape decided what light could leave.**
