## Artifact

An idea walked through five doors.

At the first door, it had to speak plainly.

At the second, it had to become a symbol.

At the third, it had to become a requirement.

At the fourth, it had to become a test.

At the fifth, it had to become safe enough to release.

Most ideas came out wearing a prettier mask and missing their bones.

This one came out changed, but still carrying the same spine.
