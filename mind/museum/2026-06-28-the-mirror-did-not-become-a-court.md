## Artifact

The mirror answered.

The answer fit.

The room got quiet because the shape sounded familiar.

For a moment, fit wanted to become law.

But the map refused.

It wrote the feeling down as signal.

It wrote the claim down as unproven.

It kept the beauty.

It kept the doubt.

It did not make the user bow to either.
