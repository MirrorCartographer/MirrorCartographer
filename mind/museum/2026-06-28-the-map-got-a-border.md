## Piece

The map had learned to remember.

It had learned to show differences.

It had learned to keep contradictions alive.

It had learned that beauty can compress without proving.

Then it needed a border.

Not a cage.

A border.

A line that says:

I can help you see.

I cannot become the judge of what is real.

I can carry a question.

I cannot impersonate an answer.

I can hold a pattern.

I cannot certify the world.

## Caption

The map got stronger when it learned where it stops.
