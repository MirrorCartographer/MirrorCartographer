## Core discovery
The strongest attractor is **Emergence**.

The emergent object is not a medical assistant. It is a **biocultural translation loop**: a system that helps private meaning become public-enough observation without losing provenance, consent, missingness, or claim boundary.

MC can affect beings biologically only through mediated pathways:

1. noticing changes earlier;
2. preserving time-order accurately;
3. reducing memory distortion;
4. translating subjective experience into professional questions;
5. improving adherence to reviewed plans;
6. coordinating multiple observers;
7. keeping privacy boundaries explicit;
8. feeding outcomes back into future observation.

The biological mechanism is therefore not mystical or direct. It is behavioral, informational, relational, and institutional.

## Architecture implication
Add a `BioculturalViewLoop` to the GitHub mind:

- `PrivateMeaningState`: unrestricted, personal, symbolic, embodied, private-first.
- `ObservationPacket`: stripped of private identity and reorganized into neutral observations.
- `ProfessionalQuestionPacket`: audience-specific questions and timelines.
- `ReviewedActionRecord`: only what a qualified professional or trusted care process actually recommends.
- `OutcomeUpdate`: what changed after the reviewed action.
- `ViewDiff`: what was omitted, generalized, transformed, or retained between views.

## Evidence alignment
Current public research supports this direction indirectly:

- AI provenance work emphasizes that transformations from source to summary need auditability.
- AI literacy work emphasizes critical evaluation rather than uncritical tool use.
- Patient-generated health data and ambient documentation work show growing demand for structured summaries, but also privacy, consent, and overreliance risks.
- Dynamic consent and privacy-preserving data transformation support the idea that access is not binary; it can be permissioned, revocable, and purpose-bound.

## Evaluation criteria
A BioculturalViewLoop pass is successful only if:

1. it improves clarity without adding unsupported claims;
2. it preserves what was changed between views;
3. it does not expose private detail unnecessarily;
4. it helps a human ask better questions;
5. it never pretends to diagnose, treat, triage, or replace professional judgment.
