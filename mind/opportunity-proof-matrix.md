## Status scale

- Role-interest: role matches MC/user interests but proof is weak.
- Evidence-building: some artifacts exist, but packet is incomplete.
- Proof-ready: artifacts can be shown without relying on novelty language.
- Application-ready: proof packet maps directly to role requirements.

## Proof packet requirement

A role is not application-ready until the packet contains:

1. role requirement;
2. matching artifact;
3. evidence of skill;
4. what the artifact proves;
5. what it does not prove;
6. one clear work sample;
7. one limitation statement.
