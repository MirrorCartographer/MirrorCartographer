# Synthetic Care-Support Fixture Boundary

## Summary
Mirror Cartographer can support care-adjacent communication only when observation, meaning, evidence, and review are separated. A synthetic fixture library should include care-support cases, but those cases must remain fictional and non-diagnostic.

## Public-Safe Fixture Rule
Care-support fixtures may test communication structure, not clinical truth.

Allowed:

- fictional observation logs
- fictional support questions
- uncertainty labels
- reviewer-required states
- examples of non-diagnostic language
- examples of escalation-boundary wording

Not allowed:

- real medical details
- real animal-care details
- treatment instructions
- diagnosis claims
- claims that a symbolic pattern proves a condition
- emergency triage claims without qualified review

## Evidence Boundary
A support fixture can show how to prepare better questions or summarize observations. It cannot decide what the observations medically mean.

## Review Rule
Any fixture that resembles health, veterinary, legal, financial, or safety-critical decision-making should route to `review` or `hold_private` unless it has been abstracted into general communication design.
