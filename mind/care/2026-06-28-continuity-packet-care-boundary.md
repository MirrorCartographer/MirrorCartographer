# Care Lane: Continuity Packet Care Boundary

Date: 2026-06-28
Attractor: Discovery
Privacy status: public-safe care boundary
Claim status: communication-support hypothesis only

## Public-safe finding
Mirror Cartographer's care-adjacent value is strongest when it improves the route from noticing to asking a better professional question. It should not claim to diagnose, treat, triage, predict outcomes, or replace licensed judgment.

## Source status
- Repository source: prior MC work defines minimum continuity packets, consent gradients, care adapters, and ViewDiff requirements.
- File-library source: mixed-sensitivity care materials reviewed only to identify abstract boundary requirements; no private details are included.
- External research: ambient documentation and patient-generated data work shows usefulness when clinician review, consent, accuracy control, and privacy boundaries remain explicit.

## Allowed use
- Organize observations.
- Separate expression from observation.
- Convert uncertainty into professional questions.
- Show what is missing.
- Preserve timing, changes, and context when consent permits.
- Produce a bounded handoff packet for review.

## Disallowed use
- Diagnosis.
- Treatment plan.
- Triage or urgency authority.
- Medication adjustment authority.
- Veterinary or medical certainty without professional review.
- Claims of healing effect.
- Public examples based on private health, household, or animal records.

## Required care-packet labels
- source_status
- claim_status
- privacy_status

- professional_review_needed
- escalation_boundary
- revision_reason

## Evaluation criteria
- Does the packet help a professional see observations faster?
- Does it avoid telling the professional what conclusion to reach?
- Does it distinguish user concern from verified fact?
- Does it preserve uncertainty instead of hiding it?
- Does it prevent oversharing?

## Missingness
No clinical validation, no veterinary validation, no social-care validation, no outcome data, no regulatory review.

## Revision reason
Created to keep the test-harness direction useful for care support while explicitly preventing unsupported clinical or veterinary claims.

## Key phrase
Care support is not authority. It is cleaner passage from lived noticing to reviewed action.
