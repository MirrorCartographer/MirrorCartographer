# Continuity Gate Support Boundary

## Purpose
Define how the continuity gate map may support medical or social-care communication without making diagnostic, therapeutic, or treatment claims.

## Support Use
The map may help convert a complex private observation history into:
- a structured observation summary
- a question list
- a timeline of changes
- a missingness register
- a review-needed note

## Boundary
The map must not diagnose, prescribe, replace professional care, or claim that symbolic patterns prove a medical or social condition.

## Evidence Lane Rule
Meaning may help organize observations. Evidence must remain lane-specific and reviewable by the relevant human or professional context.

## Source Status
Public-safe abstraction from MC architecture and current public AI documentation research.

## Claim Status
Care-communication support boundary, not medical advice.

## Privacy Status
Public-safe. No private health, household, animal-care, relationship, financial, credential, or location details included.

## Missingness
Needs fictional test fixtures and review by appropriate care, accessibility, or patient-communication specialists before public claims are expanded.

## Revision Reason
Add a safer support boundary for the continuity gate map so care-related use remains communication-focused rather than conclusion-focused.

## Key Phrase
The gate may organize what to ask. It must not pretend to know the answer.
