# Doorway Copy

## One-line version

Mirror Cartographer rebuilds understanding when important fragments have scattered across tools, time, and context.

## Short version

Most systems save information.

Mirror Cartographer asks what happens when the information is still there, but the understanding has been lost between conversations, records, notes, symbols, projects, and decisions.

This repository is the public proof layer: demonstrations, maps, boundaries, and build notes for a system that turns fragments into navigable structure.

## Strong version

Bring one thing you cannot organize.

A project.
A record set.
A symbol.
A long conversation.
A research question.
A strange repeated pattern.
A creative world.
A practical mess.

Mirror Cartographer does not promise magic.

It asks:

- what is known
- what is missing
- what connects
- what should stay uncertain
- what can be built next

## Invitation

If you recognize this problem, bring a fragment.

The map gets better when the right people test it.
