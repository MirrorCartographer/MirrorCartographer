# The Weather Inside a Bell

## Creative premise

A full-screen playable weather instrument. The visitor does not navigate information; they disturb a living field. Touch becomes pressure, pressure becomes weather, and weather rings as metal.

## Visual law

- Darkness behaves as material, not empty background.
- Every visible line must respond to force, time, or sound state.
- Typography is editorial and sparse; the generated field carries the spectacle.
- No cards, dashboards, feature grids, product funnels, explanatory diagrams, or Mirror Cartographer vocabulary.
- Interaction begins immediately and remains legible without instructions.

## Behavioral law

- Tap: strike the bell.
- Drag: bend the air and spectral center.
- Two concurrent pointers: summon rain.
- Space: keyboard bell strike.
- S: wake sound.
- Reduced-motion preference freezes ambient drift while preserving direct interaction.

## Sound law

Sound is optional, gesture-gated, and generated locally with Web Audio. It uses a low wind bed plus inharmonic bell partials. No external media, trackers, uploads, accounts, or network-dependent assets are required.

## Independence boundary

This piece is not a Mirror Cartographer interface, project explanation, research surface, worker console, evidence portal, or sales mechanism. It has no symbolic mapping system, personal data model, conversion path, or clinical/research claim. Its value is the immediate sensory behavior of the artifact itself.

## Hosting contract

`independent-web/index.html` is a zero-build static artifact. The deployment workflow publishes only this directory to GitHub Pages. Rollback is any earlier source commit; no database, secrets, migration, or irreversible state exists.
