# Afterimage Orchard

## Creative premise

A nocturnal field where pointer gestures plant temporary organisms made of light. Each organism records direction, grows once, produces luminous fruit, and eventually disappears. A storm is an event, not a content panel. Sound is optional, sparse, and gesture-activated.

## Visual law

- Darkness is spatial material, not a theme toggle.
- Marks are thin, luminous, temporary, and slightly imperfect.
- Text appears as field notes rather than navigation or explanation.
- Every interaction must alter the world directly.
- No cards, dashboards, product funnels, research summaries, symbolic self-mapping, or Mirror Cartographer terminology.

## Behavioral law

- Tap plants one light.
- Drag records direction and plants a sequence.
- Storm creates transient rain.
- Sound wakes only after an explicit gesture.
- Clearing is reversible only by making new marks.

## Accessibility and performance contract

- Core interaction works without audio.
- Controls have 44px minimum targets and keyboard focus treatment.
- Status changes use a polite live region.
- Reduced-motion preference disables ambient grain movement.
- Device pixel ratio is capped at 2.
- The artifact has no external runtime dependencies, trackers, fonts, images, or network requests.

## Independence statement

This is a self-contained generative artwork. It does not explain Mirror Cartographer, expose project operations, solicit payment, present research, map a user, or function as an agent dashboard.
