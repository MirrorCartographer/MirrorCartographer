# Contributing to Mirror Cartographer

Mirror Cartographer accepts contributions that improve clarity, continuity, provenance, accessibility, testing, and public verification.

## Contribution principles

1. Preserve the distinction between observation, interpretation, hypothesis, and verified fact.
2. Do not include private records, private conversations, identifying health information, or unredacted personal data.
3. Prefer small, reviewable changes with explicit purpose and evidence.
4. Add or update tests when behavior changes.
5. Describe uncertainty, limitations, and possible failure modes.
6. Keep symbolic interpretation optional and clearly separated from factual claims.

## Pull-request checklist

- Explain what changed and why.
- Identify affected files and interfaces.
- State how the change was tested.
- Confirm that no private or sensitive source material was added.
- Note unresolved risks or follow-up work.

## Good contribution areas

- validators and evidence gates
- accessibility and alternate interaction modes
- continuity and provenance schemas
- synthetic fixtures and regression tests
- documentation and public demonstrations
- privacy-preserving export formats

By contributing, you agree that your work may be reviewed, revised, and incorporated under the repository's applicable license and governance rules.
