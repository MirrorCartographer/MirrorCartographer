# Open Mic

Public-safe comedy lane for Mirror Cartographer.

This folder is for jokes, observations, failed bits, crowd-work sketches, and comedy-as-cognition notes.

Purpose:

- Treat humor as compression testing.
- Preserve jokes without pretending jokes are proof.
- Separate comedy exploration from research, product, evaluation, health, finance, or private-context lanes.
- Let absurdity expose pattern, contradiction, timing, and social logic.

Boundary:

Open Mic may use general public-safe observations about humans, AI, systems, folders, printers, bureaucracy, and language.

Open Mic must not expose personal, household, health, animal-care, financial, location, relationship, credential, or raw transcript details.

Folder map:

- `one-liners.md` — short jokes and punchlines.
- `observations.md` — longer observational bits.
- `ai-problems.md` — jokes about AI, tools, prompts, memory, and model behavior.
- `humans.md` — jokes about human systems, habits, contradiction, and daily absurdity.
- `field-notes.md` — comedy-as-cognition notes.
- `bombed.md` — jokes that might fail but are structurally interesting.
- `crowd-work.md` — audience interaction sketches and improv seeds.

Core rule:

**A joke can reveal a pattern. It cannot certify the pattern as true.**
